Ramen

Description

This is a vegeterian ramen recipe. This has become a staple in my house and is made often. The key to a good ramen dish is in the broth.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable broth
  • Miso paste
  • Chili oil
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Seaseme oil

Steps

  1. Mince the garlic and ginger
  2. Heat the seaseme oil in a sauce pan
  3. Add the minced garlic and ginger, cook until fragerent
  4. Add in the miso paste and cooke for 3-5 minutes
  5. Slowly stir in the vegetable broth to avoid lumps
  6. Once combined, add in the chili oil
  7. Turn the burner to low and let simmer
